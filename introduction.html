Intro

Publishing practical geospatial programs & tools.


Published


  • Flight Planning – Drone-based or large-format flight planning and kml generation tool.
  • ImageHeader2Txt – Tool to extract metadata from image header (exif and xmp) and store them in a text file.
  • Photos2GoogleEarth – Creates Kml and Kmz file to show photo location and orientation on Google Earth.
  • ATMaster Converts Roll, Pitch, Yaw -> Omega, Phi, Kappa.
  • RollPitchYaw2OmegaPhiKappa – Manual (educational) photogrammetry software. Works for small to very large-format cameras.
