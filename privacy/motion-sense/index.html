Privacy Policy for Motion Sense

Last Updated: April, 2026

GeoIntel operates the Motion Sense mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

A. Location Data

Motion Sense collects precise and approximate location data to provide tracking and mapping features (KML/CSV logging). This data is processed locally on your device and is only stored in files you choose to create.

B. Camera Access

The app requires Camera access to provide a real-time preview and capture images. Images are stored only on your device.

C. Sensor Data

We collect data from your device's Accelerometer, Magnetometer, and Gyroscope to analyze motion. This data is logged to local files (CSV) at your request.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (for advertisements)
  • Google Maps

3. Data Security

All motion and location logs are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal logs to any external servers.
