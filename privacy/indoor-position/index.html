Privacy Policy for IndoorPosition

Last Updated: April 2026

Geo-Intel ("we", "our", or "us") operates the IndoorPosition mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Executive Summary: IndoorPosition is designed as a utility tool. We do not collect or store your personal data on any external servers. Most processing happens locally on your device.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Location Data

The app requires access to your device's GNSS (GPS) location to display your position on the map and calculate trajectories. This data is used in real-time for navigation and is not transmitted to our servers.

Camera Data (ARCore)

When using the "Vision + IMU" mode, the app uses your device's camera via Google ARCore to track movement and orientation.

  • We do not record, store, or transmit any video or image data.
  • Visual data is processed locally in volatile memory to calculate relative motion.

Sensor Data

We access IMU sensors (Accelerometer, Gyroscope, Magnetometer) to provide dead-reckoning positioning. This data remains strictly on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Required for core Android functionality.
  • Google Maps: Used to display the map interface. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (like Android Advertising ID) to show relevant ads. How Google uses data
  • Google ARCore: Used for augmented reality tracking. ARCore Privacy

3. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
