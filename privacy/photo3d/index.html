Privacy Policy for Photo3D

Last Updated: April 2026

Photo3D is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use the app.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Camera Access: Photo3D requires access to your device's camera to capture photographs. Camera data is processed in real-time to provide the photography features of the app.

Location Data (GPS): The app collects precise location data (GPS coordinates) to embed geospatial information (Latitude, Longitude, Altitude) into the metadata (EXIF) of the photos you take. This is a core feature of the application for 3D reconstruction and mapping purposes.

Storage: All photos and associated log files are stored locally on your device in a folder explicitly selected by you. We do not have access to these files once they are saved.

2. Data Sharing and Transmission

No Personal Server Transmission: We do not transmit your photos, location data, or any other personal information to our servers. All geospatial processing and storage happen strictly on your device.

Third-Party Services (Google AdMob): Our app uses Google AdMob for advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data, such as your device's advertising ID and coarse location, to show relevant ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data by visiting Google's Advertising Privacy & Terms.

3. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos.
  • Location: To geo-tag your photos.
  • Storage/Files Access: To save your captured photos to your preferred directory.

4. Acceptance of Policy

By using the Photo3D application, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with these practices, please do not use the application.
