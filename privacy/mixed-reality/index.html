Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

Geo-Intel ("we", "our") operates the Mixed Reality Android application. This policy explains how we handle your data to ensure transparency and trust.

No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

1. Permissions & Usage

  • Camera: Used exclusively to provide the Augmented Reality (AR) experience by overlaying 3D models on your physical environment. We do not record, save, or transmit any images or video.
  • Location: Used to align 3D models with real-world coordinates. This data is used only for real-time positioning and never leaves your device.

2. Storage Access

The app accesses folders only when you explicitly select them via the "PICK FOLDER" button. This is used to read your 3D model files (.3dm) and coordinate files (.csv). We do not access, modify, or delete any other files.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve ads. We also provide links to YouTube for tutorial purposes. These services have their own privacy policies.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, you can contact us through our main website at geo-intel.xyz.
